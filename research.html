Research & Publications

I am an incoming PhD student at Durham University (MSCA COGENT scholar) focusing on algebraic $K$-theory. My work so far has explored the relationship between scissors congruence groups and $\mathbb{A}^1$-homotopy theory.

Research Interests

  • Algebraic $K$-theory
  • $\mathbb{A}^1$-homotopy theory
  • Scissors congruence groups
  • Milnor–Witt $K$-theory and quadratic forms
