Research & Publications

Research Interests

My research focuses on topics in algebraic K-theory. During my first master's project, I studied the fundamentals of algebraic K-theory and in my second master’s project, I studied the scissors congruence group in the context of $A^1$-homotopy theory. Specifically, I showed that any extension of it as a sheaf on the category of smooth finite type $k$-schemes (in Nisnevich topology) is not strongly $A^{1}$-invariant.
