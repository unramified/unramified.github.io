Research Interests

My research interests are in several topics in algebraic $K$-theory. During my two master's projects, I studied the fundamentals of algebraic $K$-theory and studied the scissors congruence group in the context of $A^{1}$-homotopy theory. In particular, I showed that any extension of the scissor congruence functor as a Nisnevich sheaf on the category of smooth finite-type $k$-schemes is not strongly $\mathbb{A}^{1}$-invariant.
